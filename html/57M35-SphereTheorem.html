Theorem 1 (sphere theorem).

If M is a differentiable orientable 3-manifold such that π2⁢(M) is not trivial, then there exists an embedding S2→M such that its image homotopy class is not equal to zero.

This theorem was established and proved by C. Papakyriakopoulos in 1957.

Dehn’s lemma


The Dehn’s lemma states that if a continuous map from a 2-disk (http://planetmath.org/StandardNBall) to a 3-manifold f:D2→M for which f-1⁢f⁢∂⁡D2=D2, then there exists an embedding g:D2→M such that the restrictions g|∂ and f|∂ are equal.

isotopy


Let M and N be manifolds and I=[0,1] the closed unit interval. A smooth map h:M×I→N is called an isotopy if the restriction map ht:=h⁢(-,t):M→N is an embedding for all t∈I.

In particular, a diffeotopy is an isotopy.

Remark. Given an isotopy h:M×I→N, there exists a diffeotopy g:N×I→N such that ht=gt∘h0.

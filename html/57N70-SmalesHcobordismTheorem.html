Smale’s h-cobordism theorem


Let (N,M,M′) be an h-cobordism (http://planetmath.org/HCobordism) of smooth manifolds with N (and hence M and M′) simply connected, and dim⁡(M)≥5. Then N≅M×[0,1] and M and M′ are diffeomorphic.
